# Marketplace Module (TwinVerse)

TwinVerse is the AI Twin marketplace. Every person or organization on the platform can create an AI Twin — a receptionist that represents them 24/7. The Twin knows about its creator (and only its creator), can chat with anyone who discovers them, explain what they offer, and help book calls.

Startups browse the marketplace to find mentors, investors, advisors, freelancers, clubs, and accelerators. They talk to Twins first. If there's a fit, the human gets invited to the startup's team through the existing invitation flow.
